# Contributing

This is a personal thinking space. Contributions are welcome, but the bar is alignment with the premise, not completeness or polish.

## Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). By participating — via Issues, PRs, Discussions, or otherwise — you agree to uphold it. Report unacceptable behaviour to artem.zhiganov@newspeak.house.

## Ways to engage

- **Add a reference** — PR a thinker, book, organisation, or practice into `research/README.md` with a one-line note on why.
- **Challenge a framing** — open an Issue or PR disagreeing with something in `docs/origin.md` or the concept docs. Disagreement is useful; the origin doc explicitly holds the metacrisis as a hypothesis, not a given.
- **Draft a missing doc** — `concept/principles.md`, `practices.md`, and `invitation.md` are all pending. PRs toward any of them are welcome.
- **Surface related work** — if there's another repo, community, or piece of writing this project should know about, add it to `research/` or open a discussion.

## Tone

Provisional, humble, honest. Don't fabricate claims. Flag what you're uncertain about. Preserve the "not a finished position" stance.

## Inbound license

Contributions are inbound-licensed under the same [CC BY-SA 4.0](./LICENSE) as the rest of the repo. By submitting a PR, Issue, or Discussion content you agree to this.

## Open-ended conversation

For thinking out loud rather than concrete changes, use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/zhiganov/active-solidarity/discussions).
