# Roadmap

A working horizon, not a plan. Expect it to move.

## Near-term — the seeded tracks

What's been committed and what the repo already has content to start on:

- **Principles (v0)** — Viktor's first draft of the Buddhist-philosophical principles, translated into contemporary language. ([#1](https://github.com/zhiganov/active-solidarity/issues/1))
- **Research stubs → pages** — filling out commons, metacrisis debate, principle-based organisations, embodied mind, Japanese Buddhist utopias, and Climate Fresk as individual pages under `research/`. ([#4–#9](https://github.com/zhiganov/active-solidarity/issues))
- **Concept stubs → drafts** — first passes at `practices.md` and `invitation.md`. ([#10, #11](https://github.com/zhiganov/active-solidarity/issues))
- **Cadence** — agree a rhythm for the Viktor × Artem conversation. ([#3](https://github.com/zhiganov/active-solidarity/issues/3))

## Medium-term — if the thinking coheres

These are hopes, not promises. Pursued only if the thinking holds up under pressure (principles test, not values test).

- A coherent first version of the full concept set — principles + practices + invitation, readable end-to-end.
- A small open kit — something a third party could download and run, in the spirit of [Climate Fresk](https://climatefresk.org/).
- Other people reading and pushing back — via Issues, Discussions, or forks.

## Long-term — if anything of this lands

Speculative, but grounded in what was actually described in the origin conversation:

- **Local autonomous cells** — small groups that hold the shared principles and generate their own adjacent work (ecological, contemplative, civic). Federated only via the principles, otherwise uncoupled. (Viktor, drawing on Plum Village's organisational shape.)
- **A sustaining form** — donations, grants, NGO-shape — not course-revenue or product-for-sale. (Viktor, on what he explicitly does not want to rebuild.)

## Explicitly not on the roadmap

What this is not matters as much as what it is. These are Viktor's explicit exclusions from the 2026-04-20 conversation:

- Not an online school.
- Not a teacher-around-whom-people-gather format.
- Not a course-revenue / product-for-sale shape.
- Not a closed community.
