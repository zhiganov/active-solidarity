# Origin

This repo was seeded from a conversation between Artem Zhiganov and Viktor Shiryaev on 2026-04-20.

The conversation touched on:

- Living and working in Serbia; offline community texture in Belgrade vs smaller cities
- The metacrisis as a shared diagnostic frame
- Solidarity and commons as starting points for collective response
- Contemplative / Buddhist-philosophical grounding for group life
- Offline gatherings as an antidote to digital isolation
- Climate Fresk as a worked example of facilitated collective sensemaking

Raw transcripts are intentionally kept out of this repo.
